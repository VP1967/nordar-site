Services

End-to-end technology consulting for financial services.

From infrastructure engineering to custom software — we handle the technology so our clients can focus on their business.

What We Offer
01

Infrastructure Engineering

We design and build technology infrastructure from the ground up for financial asset management firms. This includes enterprise networking, server architecture, and system integration — deployed in hybrid on-premise/cloud or fully cloud-based environments depending on the client's operational requirements.

02

Cybersecurity & Compliance

Security architecture, network hardening, access control, and ongoing monitoring tailored for financial services. We implement security frameworks that meet the compliance standards of the asset management industry while maintaining operational efficiency.

03

Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity

Planning, implementation, and testing of disaster recovery systems that ensure business continuity. We build redundancy and failover capabilities across infrastructure to minimize downtime and protect critical financial operations.

04

Telephony & Communications

Enterprise telephony solutions for financial firms, including VoIP, unified communications, and integration with trading floor systems. Deployed across hybrid and cloud-based environments with reliability as the primary concern.

05

Back-Office Workflow Design

We analyze existing back-office operations and design streamlined workflows that reduce manual effort and improve accuracy. From trade processing to reporting, we identify bottlenecks and design practical solutions that fit how teams actually work.

06

Custom Software Development

We program and implement custom software to support the workflows and operations we design. Our development work includes market data systems, trading-related software, and business applications built specifically for our clients' needs.

07

Ongoing Support & Maintenance

We don't build and walk away. Nordar provides continuous support for all infrastructure and systems we deploy — including monitoring, updates, troubleshooting, and capacity planning as our clients' needs evolve over time.

How We Work

Every engagement starts with understanding the client's business. We don't apply templates — we design solutions around the specific operational, regulatory, and technical requirements of each firm. Our work is hands-on, and we stay involved for the long term.

Whether a project requires building an entire technology environment from scratch or improving a single operational process, we bring the same level of attention and commitment to every engagement.
