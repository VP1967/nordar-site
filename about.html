About

Building technology for financial services since 2006.

Nordar, LLC is an independent software consulting company that partners with financial asset management firms to design, build, and maintain the technology that runs their business.

Our Approach

We work closely with our clients to understand their operational needs and build solutions that fit — not the other way around. Whether it's standing up an entire technology infrastructure from scratch or refining a single back-office workflow, we focus on practical, reliable results.

Our work spans the full technology stack: from networking and cybersecurity at the infrastructure level, to custom software that supports trading operations, market data systems, and back-office processes. We design for the long term and remain engaged to support what we build.

History
2006

Nordar, LLC founded as an independent software consulting company focused on fintech and financial technology services.

2006 — Present

Built ground-up technology infrastructure for financial asset management firms — including networking, cybersecurity, disaster recovery, and telephony systems across hybrid and cloud environments. In parallel, designed back-office workflows and developed custom software to support day-to-day operations, market data systems, and trading-related processes.

Today

Nordar continues to provide end-to-end technology consulting and support for the financial services industry, with a focus on practical solutions and long-term reliability.

Industry Focus

Our clients operate in financial asset management — an industry where technology reliability is not optional. We specialize in the systems and infrastructure that support trading, market data, compliance, and day-to-day operations. Every solution we build reflects the precision and dependability that financial services demand.
