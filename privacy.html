Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Nordar, LLC is committed to minimizing data collection.

Applications and Communications

Nordar, LLC does not sell personal data and does not use personal data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

For applications developed by Nordar, user-created data is intended to remain local on the user’s device unless explicitly stated otherwise. Nordar applications are not designed to collect personal data for advertising purposes.

Some Nordar applications may retrieve market data or other reference data from third-party data providers. In such cases, the provider will be clearly disclosed in the application.

If you contact Nordar directly by email or other communication channels, any information you provide is used only for responding to inquiries, support, or business-related communication.

Our website may make standard web requests necessary to serve pages securely and reliably. Nordar does not use the website to sell personal data or for cross-app tracking.
