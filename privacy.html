Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Overview

Nordar, LLC ("Nordar") is committed to minimizing data collection across all of its products and services.

General Principles

Nordar does not sell personal data. Nordar does not use personal data for advertising or cross-app tracking. Nordar products and services are not designed to collect personal data for advertising purposes.

Software Applications

For consumer applications developed by Nordar, user-created data is stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly stated otherwise. Individual applications may include their own privacy disclosures within the application that describe app-specific data practices in detail.

Professional Services

Nordar provides infrastructure and technology services to business clients. Data handling for professional engagements is governed by the terms of each individual client agreement. Nordar does not disclose client information to third parties except as required by law or as specified in the applicable agreement.

Website

The Nordar website may make standard web requests necessary to serve pages securely and reliably. Nordar does not use the website to collect personal data, serve advertising, or perform cross-site tracking.

Communications

If you contact Nordar by email or other communication channels, any information you provide is used only for responding to your inquiry or for related business communication.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact contact@nordarllc.com.
